Milkshake

Description

Reminiscent of countertop service and carhop days, there is nothing better than a perfect milkshake. Luckily, making one at home is extremely easy.
This easy milkshake recipe gives you the perfect ratio of milk to ice cream and is completely customizable!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream scoops * 4
  • Milk
  • Whipped topping
  • Sprinkle
  • Maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
